Our Mission

FLORES empowers Latine organizers in suburban communities by fostering vital connections, providing popular education, and promoting community service. We cultivate spaces for growth, cultural affirmation, and collective action, rooted in a deep respect for Pachamama, to build a more just and vibrant future.

Peciolos

Our work is organized into several key areas, our "peciolos," each focusing on a vital aspect of community empowerment.
